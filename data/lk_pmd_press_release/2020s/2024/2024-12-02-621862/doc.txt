Ms. K.D.R. Olga Appointed as Secretary to the Ministry of Women and Child Affairs
President Anura Kumara Dissanayake has officially appointed Ms. K.D.R. Olga as the Secretary to the Ministry of Women and Child Affairs.
The appointment letter was formally presented to Ms. Olga by the Secretary to the President, Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake today (02) at the Presidential Secretariat.
