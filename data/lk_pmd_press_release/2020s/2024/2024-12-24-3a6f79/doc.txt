Christmas Carol Concert at Presidential Secretariat Enters Second Day
The special annual Christmas Carol Concert, organized jointly by the Presidential Secretariat, the Tri-Forces, and the Police, held its second day yesterday (23) within the Presidential Secretariat premises.
The event began amidst the vibrant illumination of the Secretariat premises and surrounding areas. Yesterday’s performance featured musical and choral presentations by the Sri Lanka Air Force band and choir.
Today (24), the Sri Lanka Air Force band and choir are also scheduled to perform at the Christmas Carol Concert.
This festive event will continue daily until December 25, starting at 7:00 PM at the Presidential Secretariat premises.
