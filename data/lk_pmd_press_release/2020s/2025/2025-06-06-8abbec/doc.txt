K.G.P. Pushpakumara Appointed Chief Secretary of the Western Province
President Anura Kumara Disanayake has appointed Mr K.G.P. Pushpakumara as the Chief Secretary of the Western Province.
The official letter of appointment was handed over to Mr Pushpakumara today (6) at the Presidential Secretariat by Secretary to the President, Dr Nandika Sanath Kumanayake.
