Dr. Muditha Darshana Senarath Yapa Appointed Chief Innovation Officer of National Innovation Agency
President Anura Kumara Disanayake has appointed Dr. Muditha Darshana Senarath Yapa as the new Chief Innovation Officer of the National Innovation Agency.
The official letter of appointment was handed over to Dr. Yapa this afternoon (20) at the Presidential Secretariat by Secretary to the President Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake.
