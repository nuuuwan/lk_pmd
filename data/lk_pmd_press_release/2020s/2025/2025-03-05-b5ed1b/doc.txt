New Secretary Appointed to the Ministry of Buddhasasana, Religious and Cultural Affairs
President Anura Kumara Dissanayake has appointed W. P. Senadheera as the new Secretary to the Ministry of Buddhasasana, Religious and Cultural Affairs.
The official appointment letter was handed over to W. P. Senadheera by the Secretary to the President Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake today (05) at the Presidential Secretariat.
