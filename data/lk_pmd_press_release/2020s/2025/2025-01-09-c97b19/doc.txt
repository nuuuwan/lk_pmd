M.L. Gammampila Appointed as Chief Secretary of the Uva Province
President Anura Kumara Disanayake has appointed M.L. Gammampila as the Chief Secretary of the Uva Province.
The official appointment letter was handed over to Mr Gammampila this morning (9) at the Presidential Secretariat by Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake, Secretary to the President.
