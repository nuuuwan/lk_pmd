The President is scheduled to meet with the Ambassadors and High Commissioners of seven countries today

President Anura Kumara Dissanayake is scheduled to engage in talks today (02) with ambassadors, high commissioners, and representatives from seven countries.

In line with the current government’s policy of strengthening bilateral relations globally, the President will meet with these foreign diplomats and representatives for discussions.

The ambassadors, high commissioners, and representatives from Cuba, the United Kingdom, Australia, China, Japan, Canada, and India are expected to participate in these meetings.
