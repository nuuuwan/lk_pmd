German Foreign Minister calls on President Disanayake
During his official visit to Germany, the German Foreign Minister Dr. Johann Wadephul called on President Anura Kumara Disanayake last evening (11) at the Waldorf Astoria Hotel in Berlin.
The discussion focused on identifying new avenues for expanding bilateral cooperation, particularly in the areas of trade, the digital economy, investment and vocational training, in line with the priorities of the Sri Lankan government.
