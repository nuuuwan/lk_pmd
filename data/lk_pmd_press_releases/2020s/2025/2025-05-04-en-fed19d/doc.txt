President Meets General Secretary of the Communist Party of Vietnam
President Anura Kumara Disanayake, currently on a state visit to Vietnam, met with the General Secretary of the Communist Party of Vietnam (CPV), Tô Lâm, at the Party’s Central Committee Headquarters in Hanoi this afternoon (04)
The discussion focused on strengthening the longstanding diplomatic relations between Vietnam and Sri Lanka, which mark 55 years of bilateral ties.
Minister of Foreign Affairs, Foreign Employment and Tourism Vijitha Herath, also participated in the meeting.
