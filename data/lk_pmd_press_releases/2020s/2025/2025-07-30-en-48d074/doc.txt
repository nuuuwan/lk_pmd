90-Day Free On-Arrival Tourist Visa for Sri Lankan Citizens Visiting the Maldives

In conjunction with the state visit of President Anura Kumara Dissanayake to the Maldives, the Government of the Maldives has announced that Sri Lankan citizens traveling to the Maldives for tourism purposes will be granted a free 90-day on-arrival tourist visa.

This visa facility will take effect from 29th July 2025, and to obtain the visa, travellers must possess a valid passport and proof of sufficient financial means to cover their expenses during their stay in the Maldives.

This initiative is offered under the provisions of the bilateral agreement on visa facilitation between Sri Lanka and the Maldives, and in accordance with Maldivian immigration laws and regulations. The Maldivian Government stated that this action demonstrates its strong dedication to improving mutual cooperation and reinforces the importance it places on its relationship with Sri Lanka.
