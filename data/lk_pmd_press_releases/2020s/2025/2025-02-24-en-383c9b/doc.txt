Prasanna Perera Appointed as Director General of the President’s Media Division
Marking a new chapter in the government’s communication strategy, Mr. Prasanna Perera has been appointed as the Director General of the President’s Media Division (PMD),
The official letter of appointment was handed over to Mr. Perera today (24) by the Secretary to the President Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake at the Presidential Secretariat.
Bringing a wealth of academic expertise to the role, Mr. Perera holds a Bachelor of Arts degree from the University of Peradeniya, along with a Postgraduate Diploma in Communication from the University of Sri Jayewardenepura and a Master Degree in Mass Communication from the University of Kelaniya.
