Special State Banquet in Honour of the President

President of the Maldives hosted a state banquet in honour of President Anura Kumara Dissanayake, who is currently on a state visit to the Maldives, last night (28) at the Kurumba resort, Maldives.

The dinner was organized by Maldivian President Dr. Mohamed Muizzu.

The Sri Lankan delegation, including the Minister of Foreign Affairs, Foreign Employment and Tourism, Vijitha Herath, participated in this occasion.
