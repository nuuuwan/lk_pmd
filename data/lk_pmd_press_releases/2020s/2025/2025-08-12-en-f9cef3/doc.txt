Meeting between President and Australian High Commissioner

President Anura Kumara Dissanayake met with the Australian High Commissioner to Sri Lanka, His Excellency Paul Stephens, at the Presidential Secretariat this afternoon (12).

Extending his best wishes as Mr. Stephens prepares to conclude his term in Sri Lanka, the President expressed appreciation for the High Commissioner’s support during and after the country’s recent economic crisis. He also commended Mr. Stephens’ efforts to strengthen bilateral ties, noting the visits of several Australian diplomats to Sri Lanka during his tenure.

High Commissioner Stephens reaffirmed Australia’s commitment to supporting Sri Lanka’s development, particularly in the areas of tourism, education and vocational training.

Also present at the meeting were Dr. Nandika Sanath Kumanayake, Secretary to the President and Mr. Roshan Gamage, Senior Additional Secretary to the President.
